# 2025 Year in Review

## Meetups

- 2025 Kickoff (January 8, 2025) A planning session for the year, covering the AI Symposium, HudsonAlpha Tech Challenge (HATCH), and Huntsville BSides Conference.

- AI for Code Development (January 22, 2025) A discussion on AI tools for code development, comparing free and paid options like GitHub Copilot, Cursor, and self-hosted models.

- Virtual Paper Review: Reasoning Models (February 12, 2025) A review of the foundation of reasoning models, covering Chain-of-Thought and DeepSeek's V3, R1, and Math papers.

- Deep Research (February 19, 2025) A session focused on "Deep Research," likely covering OpenAI's tool and other research-generation agents.

- AI Agent Frameworks (April 16, 2025) A deep dive into AI Agent frameworks like smolagents, LlamaIndex, and LangGraph, as well as protocols like MCP and A2A.

- Paper Review: Wan Video Gen Model (April 23, 2025) A virtual review session covering the Wan video generation model paper.

- Virtual Paper Review: Google Prompt Engineering Guide (May 14, 2025) A discussion centered on Google's updated Prompt Engineering guide to explore advancements in prompting techniques like Chain of Thought and ReAct.

- Cline + OpenRouter (May 28, 2025) A walkthrough of using the Cline plugin for Visual Studio Code connected via OpenRouter to assist with development and task management.

- Virtual Paper Review: Deception & Alignment (June 18, 2025) A review of research regarding deception and alignment faking in agentic AI models.

- Passing AI Certification Exams (June 25, 2025) A presentation by Tom Plunkett covering strategies for passing AI certification exams, with a specific focus on NVIDIA's Generative AI and Multimodal exams.

- Summer Social (July 9, 2025) A social networking event held at Yellowhammer/Stovehouse.

- Replit AI Agent (July 23, 2025) A session featuring Jack Francis demonstrating how to use Replit's AI Agent to build an application from scratch using simple prompts.

- SMD Symposium Field Trip (August 6, 2025) A field trip to the Space & Missile Defense Symposium to identify and visit companies displaying AI-related projects.

- Virtual Paper Review (August 13, 2025) The monthly virtual paper review session.

- Group Project Roundup (August 20, 2025) A session dedicated to discussing and reviewing group projects.

- AI 201 (September 16, 2025) A special session supporting the AI Huntsville Task Force, providing a walkthrough of AI tools for strategy, research, and content generation using a retail marketing use case.

- Lightning Talks (September 17, 2025) A series of short, rapid-fire presentations on various AI topics, including AI Hackathons and updates on translation services.

- Virtual Paper Review: Structured Agentic Software Engineering (October 8, 2025) A review of the "SE 3.0" paper, unpacking how to organize human-AI teamwork with coordinated workspaces and lightweight scripts.

- Fall Social (October 15, 2025) A social meetup at Stovehouse to connect with the community.

- Engineering AI Agents (November 5, 2025) A practical demonstration of a complete agentic engineering workbench using tools like Pydantic-AI, Arize Phoenix, and Claude Code.

- Virtual Paper Review (November 12, 2025) The monthly virtual paper review session.

- AI Impacts in the Medical Field (November 19, 2025) A discussion on the applications and impacts of AI within the healthcare and medical industries.

- 2025 Year in Review (December 17, 2025) The final meetup of the year to round up topics covered in 2025 and look forward to plans for 2026.
